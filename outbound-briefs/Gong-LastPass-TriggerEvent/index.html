    Outbound Strategic Research Brief — Sumble → Gong¶

    Date: 2026-05-06 Target (whom Sumble is pitching): Gong (gong.io) Anchor prospect (whom Gong should pursue): LastPass [WIN-BACK + EXPANSION] Recommended recipient at Gong: Kyle Groff — VP Revenue Transformation (multi-thread Emily He, CMO)

    TL;DR¶

    Sumble surfaces a $500K–$800K ACV expansion deal at LastPass invisible to ZoomInfo / Apollo / 6Sense / Clari: new CRO Chris Michelmore (Feb 2026, ex-Zoom), 3 senior GTM hires under him in 60 days, dormant Gong adoption (last Gong-tagged job 18 months ago, 0 active people). 50–100 lookalikes per quarter = ~$25M qualifying pipeline / quarter, $25M annualized new ARR — about 30% of Gong's 2026 net-new ARR target from a single play.

    1. Business Problem Sumble Solves For Gong¶

    Gong publicly committed to $300M ARR + 28% YoY growth in March 2025 — top-quartile public-SaaS trajectory. 28% on $300M = ~$84M net-new ARR for 2026, which at 4× pipeline coverage and 25% win rate requires ~$336M of qualified pipeline to materialize.

    Legacy firmographic stack. Light coverage (much lighter than Zendesk-scale benchmarks). Missing the modern signal layer entirely.

    Their existing tools tell them who exists and an intent score. None surface team-level revenue-org rebuild patterns: dormant tooling adoption, new-CRO 60-day team-building, prior-employer Gong-DNA, channel-vs-direct-vs-enablement structure. Those signals are only visible in Sumble — and they are exactly what Gong's plays (new-CRO, GTM hiring surge, product launch, CRM migration) need to fire reliably.

    2. Anchor Prospect: LastPass [WIN-BACK + EXPANSION]¶

    LastPass — identity / password management, B2B + B2C, ~1,500–2,000 employees post-LogMeIn spin-off, estimated $200–300M ARR.

    Note: default in the methodology is [NEW LOGO]. LastPass is flagged [WIN-BACK + EXPANSION] because the dormant-Gong + new-CRO-from-Zoom signal density is uniquely Gong-shaped; pure new-logo alternatives can be queued if preferred.

    3. Why LastPass Fits the Trophy Pattern¶

    Gong's trophies are mid-large B2B SaaS / enterprise tech: HubSpot, ADP, LinkedIn, SurveyMonkey, Sprout Social, PitchBook. LastPass matches the size + B2B-tech shape and is at the exact inflection — revenue-architecture rebuild under a new CRO — when Gong lands hardest.

    4. Financial Frame (Segment-by-Segment)¶

    Anchor deal at LastPass¶

    • ~70 active Salesforce-tagged users + new Channel Sales rebuild → estimated 200–300 sales / CS / channel seats
    • Gong Enterprise bundle (Engage + Forecast + Enable) at ~$1,500–2,500/seat/year
    • Estimated anchor ACV: $500K–$800K (recurring)

    Net-new lookalike TAM for the "new CRO at B2B SaaS scale-up" play¶

    • ~50–100 mid-large B2B SaaS appoint a CRO per quarter (Pavilion's "40 CROs to Watch" + similar industry tracking)
    • Avg ACV at this segment: $400–600K
    • 50 lookalike accounts × $500K avg = $25M qualifying pipeline per quarter
    • 25% win rate = $6.25M new ARR / quarter = $25M annualized

    → ~30% of Gong's 2026 net-new ARR target ($84M) from a single Sumble-surfaced play.

    Trigger 1 (the bullseye): new CRO Chris Michelmore appointed Feb 2026¶

    Came from Zoom — where Gong is heavily deployed. He knows Gong's value firsthand. First quarter in seat = setting strategy and tooling baseline.

    Trigger 2: 3 senior GTM hires under Michelmore in 60 days¶

    Michele Prashaw VP Sales Operations March 2026

    Trigger 3: Dormant Gong instance¶

    Gong is in their tech-stack history (4 jobs / 5 teams) — but last visible Gong job is November 2024 (18 months stale) with 0 active Gong-tagged people. Clari (3 jobs / 2 teams) also went stale at the same time. Both revenue-intel platforms went dormant during the LogMeIn spin-off / leadership churn. A new CRO who ran Gong at Zoom is uniquely positioned to re-activate.

    Why the window will close¶

    Michelmore has 90–180 days to set the team's tooling baseline before quarterly results lock him in. Get in now or wait a year.

    🥇 Chris Michelmore — Chief Revenue Officer (Feb 2026, Castle Rock, CO). Primary buyer. Ex-Zoom — he doesn't need a Gong demo, he needs the deployment plan.

    🥈 Michele Prashaw — VP Sales Operations (March 2026, Denver). Champion / operationalizer who will run the eval and own the integration.

    🥉 Dave Allison — VP Worldwide Channel Sales (Feb 2026, Reno). Stakeholder for the channel-conversation-intel use case. Multi-thread.

    7. What Sumble Surfaced (That ZoomInfo / Apollo / 6Sense / Clari Cannot)¶

    • Dormant-Gong + new-CRO combo. ZoomInfo can show LastPass's headcount and that they had Gong. It cannot show the adoption decay timeline (last Gong job 18 months ago, 0 active people) crossed with the new CRO who came from a Zoom-shaped Gong-customer org. Sumble does this through team-level tech adoption tracking with timestamped job-post evidence.
    • The 60-day rebuild pattern. Apollo and 6Sense intent-score on web behavior. They can't see "CRO + VP Sales Ops + VP Channel Sales + Director Americas Channel hired in 60 days." Tooling-decision urgency only shows up in team-level hiring patterns. Sumble identifies this via job-post graph clustering.
    • Zoom-DNA on Michelmore. LinkedIn Sales Navigator can show his prior role. It cannot tag "this CRO came from a Gong-customer org" as an actionable Sumble signal. Sumble's tech-adoption-by-prior-employer mapping makes this queryable.
    • Dormant-Clari overlap. They had Gong AND Clari historically — both went stale together. Consolidation-under-new-leadership signal. Sumble surfaces this via cross-vendor adoption decay.

    8. The Pitch Gong Should Run¶

    Lead: "Welcome to the seat, Chris. You ran Gong at Zoom — you know the productivity story. Your team here is scaling under a new structure (Allison on channel, Prashaw on ops). Worth 25 minutes to scope a re-activation with the channel motion as the wedge?"

    Proof points: Gong's ADP, HubSpot, LinkedIn case studies (B2B-at-scale rebuilds) plus Gong's own ROI data on channel-sales-intel specifically.

    CTA: 25-minute scoping call with Michelmore + Prashaw, multi-thread Allison on channel use case. 90-day re-activation pilot scoped to the channel sales motion (limits scope, builds momentum, sets up enterprise-tier expansion in renewal cycle).

    Methodology — how Sumble's MCP found this¶

    Phase-by-phase, ~350 credits total:

    1. GetMyCompanyProfile (free) — grounded Sumble's persona/play context
    2. WebFetch gong.io — extracted trophy customers (HubSpot, ADP, LinkedIn, SurveyMonkey, Sprout Social, PitchBook), spear-tip product (Gong Agents + Forethought partnership), and Industry Expansion / PE GTM strategy
    3. EnrichOrganization on gong.io filtered to Sumble competitors + complementary tools — mapped 12-tool legacy stack, identified missing modern signal layer (no Clay / Common Room / UserGems)
    4. FindJobs on gong.io — surfaced Industry Expansion (IX) team, Revenue Architecture function, AI Framework engineering investment
    5. FindPeople on gong.io — built persona shortlist (Emily He CMO, Kyle Groff VP Revenue Transformation, Dana Feldman VP Enterprise Revenue Unit Feb 2026, Matt Flotard VP RevOps Nov 2025)
    6. WebSearch — found Gong's $300M ARR / 28% growth / IPO commentary, plus three recent CRO appointments at B2B SaaS (LastPass, OpenObserve, Nudge Security)
    7. EnrichOrganization on lastpass.com — uncovered dormant Gong + dormant Clari pattern, active Salesforce + Outreach + Marketo, 6Sense and ZoomInfo on legacy track
    8. FindJobs on lastpass.com — confirmed 60-day rebuild pattern under new CRO (Allison, Prashaw, Director Americas Channel Sales)
    9. FindPeople on lastpass.com — verified Chris Michelmore (CRO, Feb 2026, ex-Zoom)

    Total elapsed time: ~3 minutes. ~350 credits.
