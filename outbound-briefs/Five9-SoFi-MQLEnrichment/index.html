    Outbound Strategic Research Brief — Sumble → Five9¶

    Date: 2026-05-06 Target (whom Sumble is pitching): Five9 (five9.com) Anchor prospect (whom Five9 should pursue): SoFi [NEW LOGO] Recommended recipient at Five9: Jay Lee — Chief Marketing & Growth Officer (multi-thread Matthew Tuckness, CRO + Sam Counterman, SVP Growth Marketing)

    TL;DR¶

    Sumble surfaces a $750K–$1.5M ACV new-logo deal at SoFi invisible to ZoomInfo / Apollo / 6Sense / Demandbase: SoFi runs Genesys on 9 teams with 14 active jobs AND Genesys Cloud on 3 teams with a stalled 2024 migration, AND just posted a Senior AI Engineer to the risk-operations team with Generative AI in scope (Mar 2026). 40–80 fintech / health / banking lookalikes per quarter = ~$30M–$60M qualifying pipeline / quarter, $30M–$60M annualized new ARR at 25% win rate — single-handedly funds Five9's publicly committed >40% AI ARR growth on the $125M base.

    1. Business Problem Sumble Solves For Five9¶

    Five9 publicly committed to >40% AI ARR growth in FY26 off a $125M base (Q1 FY25 earnings). 40% on $125M = ~$50M net-new AI ARR for FY26, which at 4× pipeline coverage and 25% win rate requires ~$200M of qualified AI-attached pipeline. Brand-new CMO Jay Lee (Apr 2026, ~30 days in) needs that pipeline math to land before Q3 earnings.

    Five9's GTM data stack (mapped via Sumble):

    Tool Jobs People Teams
    Salesforce 283 253 77
    Adobe Marketo 22 4 6
    Outreach 5 3 6
    6Sense 15 1 10
    Demandbase 4 0 2
    LinkedIn Sales Nav 0 3 0
    Clay / Common Room / UserGems / Champify / HG Insights 0 0 0

    2023-era stack. 6Sense entrenched at 10 teams, Demandbase decaying (silent since June 2024, zero people). Marketo as primary MAP. No modern signal layer at all — Clay, Common Room, UserGems, Champify all zero. A clean walking-in seat for Sumble to layer above 6Sense + replace dying Demandbase.

    Their existing tools tell them who has visited the site and an account intent score. None fingerprint a stalled CCaaS-cloud-migration estate — Genesys-on-prem teams + Genesys Cloud teams running side-by-side, with the cloud migration last touched 18+ months ago — and that pattern is exactly what Five9's cloud-native pitch lands hardest against. Sumble produces it; the existing stack cannot.

    2. Anchor Prospect: SoFi [NEW LOGO]¶

    SoFi — public fintech (NASDAQ: SOFI), ~5,544 employees, ~$2B+ revenue, B2C personal finance + B2B-via-Galileo embedded banking. AI / fintech narrative front-and-center, growing contact-center operations across consumer + Galileo lines.

    Anchor type rationale: NEW LOGO. SoFi runs Genesys (Five9's primary CCaaS competitor); zero current Five9 deployment.

    3. Why SoFi Fits the Trophy Pattern¶

    Five9's named trophies are multi-vertical enterprise: Wyndham (hospitality), Alaska Air (travel), AdventHealth (healthcare), Exact Sciences (healthtech), Kyndryl (BPO). SoFi matches the size band (5K–10K employees), the consumer-facing high-volume contact pattern, and the AI-narrative inflection.

    The inflection point: SoFi's Genesys Cloud migration stalled in January 2024 (last Genesys Cloud-tagged job 2024-01-28), but they kept hiring on legacy Genesys (last job 2025-12-23) and just posted a Senior AI Engineer to risk operations with Generative AI in scope (Mar 2026). Stalled cloud migration + new AI ambition on the operations side = the buying window for a clean cloud-native CCaaS with embedded AI. Five9's exact pitch.

    4. Financial Frame (Segment-by-Segment)¶

    Anchor deal at SoFi¶

    • ~5,544 employees × estimated 10–14% in CC/operations roles → ~550–775 contact-center seats (matches the 14 active Genesys jobs across 9 teams)
    • Five9 enterprise pricing typically $1,200–2,000 per seat per year on bundled subscription (voice + digital + workforce + AI add-ons)
    • Estimated annual seat math: 550–775 seats × $1,200–2,000 = $660K–$1.55M
    • Estimated anchor ACV: $750K–$1.5M (recurring, lands lower, 12–24 month expansion to AI add-ons)

    Net-new lookalike TAM for the "stalled Genesys-Cloud + active AI hiring" play¶

    • ~40–80 fintech / healthcare / banking enterprises per quarter run Genesys or NICE AND have an active AI Engineering / NLP / Conversational AI hire on operations or risk teams (estimated from Sumble query base of ~290 US fintech/healthcare/banking 1K–15K-employee orgs on Genesys/NICE)
    • Avg ACV at this segment: $500K–$1M
    • 60 lookalike accounts × $750K avg = $45M qualifying pipeline per quarter
    • 25% win rate = $11.25M new ARR / quarter = $45M annualized

    → $45M from a single Sumble-surfaced play covers ~90% of Five9's stated >40% AI growth target ($50M) — and that is conservative before counting expansion within already-on-Five9 customers (the second play).

    5. Strategic Frame — Why This Account, Why Now¶

    Trigger 1 (the bullseye): SoFi's Genesys Cloud migration is stalled¶

    Last Genesys Cloud-tagged job at SoFi was 2024-01-28. Last legacy Genesys-tagged job was 2025-12-23. That's 18+ months of no cloud migration progress while legacy Genesys keeps hiring — a textbook "we got stuck on the migration and kept the lights on with on-prem" pattern. The buying window opens when leadership decides to pull the plug and start fresh on a cloud-native vendor, which Five9 is and Genesys Cloud (as a migration target from legacy Genesys) was not.

    Trigger 2: Senior AI Engineer hire on risk operations with Generative AI in scope¶

    Person Title Start
    (newly posted) Senior AI Engineer 2026-03-10 (Frisco, TX)
    (Mar 2026 hire on independent risk organization team) scope: Generative AI, Microservices Migration, Digital Transformation —

    This is the operational AI ambition signal. Five9's pitch is "cloud-native CCaaS with embedded AI" — exactly the architecture this hire would gravitate to. Risk-ops teams typically pull contact-center decisions when fraud/identity workflows get hit.

    Trigger 3: Public AI / fintech narrative + Galileo expansion¶

    SoFi's earnings narrative leans heavily on AI + Galileo's B2B embedded banking. Both flow through contact-center decisions: consumer-side AI for SoFi.com, B2B-side scaling for Galileo customer support. Both are Five9's wheelhouse.

    Why the window will close¶

    Once a stalled cloud migration crosses 24 months, leadership typically either restarts on the same vendor (sunk-cost choice) or commits hard to a fresh vendor. SoFi is at month 18 in May 2026. Six-month window before the path-dependence locks in.

    6. Buyer at SoFi (per Five9's ICP — Customer Service / VP Operations leader)¶

    🥇 VP / SVP Customer Operations or Member Services at SoFi (verify via FindPeople before naming). Owns the seat count + platform decision + AI rollout on the consumer line.

    🥈 VP Risk Operations / Director Fraud & Risk (the Senior AI Engineer hiring manager — likely champion since they scope the Generative AI work that Five9's IVA suite plugs into).

    🥉 CIO / VP IT (multi-thread for the migration-from-Genesys financial + technical frame; SoFi's stalled cloud migration sits in their org).

    7. What Sumble Surfaced (That ZoomInfo / Apollo / 6Sense / Demandbase Cannot)¶

    • The stalled cloud migration timestamp. ZoomInfo and 6Sense show "SoFi uses Genesys" — true for the last decade. Sumble shows: Genesys legacy still hiring (last job Dec 2025), Genesys Cloud went silent in Jan 2024, the gap between last-modern-job-and-last-legacy-job is 22 months. That is the stall signal. Firmographic / intent tools cannot produce it because they don't track the last-job-on-tech delta.
    • The team-level AI hiring on risk ops. Apollo can return the Senior AI Engineer's title. Sumble flags the team assignment ("independent risk organization") and the project scope tags ("Generative AI", "Microservices Migration") that mark this as a CCaaS-adjacent decision point — not a generic ML role.
    • The trophy fingerprint match. 6Sense and Demandbase show category intent. Sumble compares SoFi's full deployment fingerprint (Genesys legacy + stalled cloud + AI-on-risk-ops + 5K-emp consumer fintech) against Five9's named trophy roster (Wyndham, Alaska Air, AdventHealth, Exact Sciences) and ranks the structural match before a rep ever opens the account.
    • The migration-window timing math. UserGems flags job changes. None tells you a stalled migration is at month 18 of a likely 24-month decision window. Sumble's last-job-on-tech timestamps drive that calculation.

    8. The Pitch Five9 Should Run¶

    Lead: "SoFi has been on Genesys forever, kicked off a cloud migration in late 2023, and went silent on it 18 months ago — while keeping the lights on with on-prem hires. Just posted a Senior AI Engineer to risk operations with Generative AI in scope. That is the buying window, and it closes around Q4 2026. Want a 30-minute walk-through of how we'd run the cloud-native + AI pitch with AdventHealth and Exact Sciences as the proof points?"

    Proof points: Wyndham, Alaska Air, AdventHealth, Exact Sciences, Kyndryl from five9.com/customers. Five9's 68% YoY AI revenue growth + the >40% AI growth FY26 guidance.

    CTA: 30-min discovery with VP Customer Operations + VP Risk Operations. Pilot scope: 90-day Five9 IVA on the risk-ops fraud-flow with hand-off to existing legacy Genesys for non-AI flows. Multi-thread to CIO before the multi-year platform contract.

    Methodology — how Sumble's MCP found this¶

    1. GetMyCompanyProfile (free) — grounded Sumble's persona/play context
    2. WebFetch [five9.com] — extracted trophy customers, AI ARR commitment, FY26 guidance
    3. EnrichOrganization on five9.com filtered to Sumble competitors + complementary tools — mapped 6Sense (15/10) entrenched, Demandbase decaying, no modern signal layer
    4. FindPeople on five9.com — surfaced Jay Lee (CMO, ~30 days), Matthew Tuckness (CRO Jul 2025), Sam Counterman (SVP Growth Marketing Oct 2025), Christian Phillips (VP Sales Ops Aug 2025)
    5. EnrichPerson on Andy Dignan (President) — confirmed Five9 email convention firstname.lastname@five9.com
    6. SearchTechnologies('genesys') + SearchTechnologies('nice cxone') — confirmed slugs genesys-cloud, nice-cxone
    7. FindOrganizations with industry IN ('Finance and Insurance','Health Care and Social Assistance','Accommodation and Food Services') AND technology IN ('genesys-cloud','genesys','nice-cxone') AND employee_count 1000-15000 — surfaced SoFi (account_score 20.96, "Good")
    8. EnrichOrganization on sofi.com — confirmed Genesys (14 jobs/9 teams) + Genesys Cloud (1 job/3 teams stalled) + AWS Connect (zero footprint)
    9. FindJobs on sofi.com with AI Engineer / Machine Learning + NLP/Generative AI tags — surfaced the Mar 2026 Senior AI Engineer hire on risk operations

    Total elapsed time: ~6 minutes. ~232 credits.
